# Shopify Store Support — Knowledge Base

## Account & Login

### How do I reset my password?
Go to your Shopify admin login page and click "Forgot password?" Enter the email address associated with your account. You'll receive a password reset link within a few minutes. If you don't see it, check your spam folder.

### How do I change my store email?
Log in to your Shopify admin. Go to Settings > General. Under "Store details," update your email address and click Save.

### I'm locked out of my account. What do I do?
If you've been locked out after too many login attempts, wait 30 minutes and try again. If you still can't get in, contact us with your store URL and we'll verify your identity and help you regain access.

## Orders & Shipping

### How do I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. Click the tracking link or enter the number on the carrier's website (UPS, FedEx, USPS, etc.) to see real-time updates.

### Can I cancel or modify an order?
You can cancel or modify an order only if it hasn't been fulfilled yet. Go to Orders in your Shopify admin, find the order, and click "Cancel order" or edit the items. If it's already shipped, you'll need to process a return instead.

### What is the return policy?
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in their original packaging. To start a return, go to your order confirmation email and click "Return items," or contact our support team.

### How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping takes 2-3 business days. International orders may take 10-15 business days depending on customs processing.

## Payments & Billing

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Shop Pay. Some regions may have additional local payment options.

### Why was my payment declined?
Common reasons include insufficient funds, incorrect card details, or your bank blocking the transaction. Try a different payment method or contact your bank to authorize the charge.

### How do I get a refund?
Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days after we receive your returned item. The refund will go back to your original payment method. You'll receive an email confirmation once the refund is issued.

## Products & Inventory

### How do I know if an item is in stock?
Product pages show real-time inventory status. If an item says "In Stock," it's available for immediate shipping. If it says "Out of Stock," you can sign up for restock notifications.

### Do you offer gift cards?
Yes! We offer digital gift cards in denominations of $25, $50, $100, and $200. Gift cards are delivered via email and never expire.

### Can I get a discount on bulk orders?
Yes, we offer bulk pricing for orders of 10+ items. Contact our support team with your order details and we'll provide a custom quote.

## Technical Support

### My Shopify store is loading slowly. What can I do?
Try optimizing your images (use compressed formats like WebP), reducing the number of apps installed, and using a fast Shopify theme. You can also enable lazy loading for images in your theme settings.

### How do I add a custom domain?
Go to Settings > Domains in your Shopify admin. Click \"Buy new domain\" or \"Connect existing domain.\" Follow the instructions to update your DNS settings. Changes may take up to 48 hours to propagate.

### How do I install an app?
Visit the Shopify App Store, find the app you want, and click \"Add app.\" You'll be redirected to authorize the app's permissions. Once approved, the app will appear in your Shopify admin sidebar.
